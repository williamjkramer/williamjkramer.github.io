An Estate Planning Attorney for Your Peace of Mind

Proudly Serving Clients Throughout Springfield

Welcome to William J Kramer, L.L.C., where we combine experience and a personal approach to estate planning and estate administration. We understand that many Springfields believe estate planning is only for the rich, but we know better. Virtually everyone owns something, such as a car, a checking account or furniture. This means that virtually everyone can benefit from estate planning!

Because every situation is unique to the individual, one size never fits all when it comes to estate planning. Attorney William J. Kramer will work with you to detail your wishes for your estate with a professionally prepared will, revocable living trust or other planning strategy. Our teams goal is to provide practical advice, responsive counsel and ultimately peace of mind.

We are proud to serve residents of the following communities:

  • Springfield
  • Springfield
  • Bloomington
  • Eden Prairie
  • Minnetonka
  • And more

What Sets Our Firm Apart

  • Comprehensive Estate Planning Services We offer every service needed to protect your legacy and your loved ones with proactive estate planning. From the simplest will to the most complex planning strategy involving Irrevocable Life Insurance Trusts, Personal Residence Trusts, Family Limited Partnerships or Limited Liability Companies, our goal is always to help you protect the people and things you love most.
  • Passion Joel is passionate about estate planning because he has seen too many families torn apart by conflict over assets. Its why he exclusively practices as an estate planning attorney, and why he frequently speaks on family conflict resolution.
  • Responsive Client Service Our approach to every client is a personal one. Joel will spend a significant amount of time understanding your needs and wants and answering your questions before crafting your estate plan. We will ensure you are comfortable and confident with the results of our work.

Schedule a Consultation Today

You and your loved ones deserve to enjoy all the benefits of estate planning. If you live in greater Springfield, contact William J Kramer, L.L.C. at (815) 780 2928 or complete our online form today to get started. We would be honored to provide you with effective estate planning services.
